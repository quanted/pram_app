Services to Human Well-Being Dashboard




Twenty-two economic, ecosystem, and social service scores were calculated for each county in the conterminous United States. Choose your county then adjust the services sliders to examine how changing services affects domain values and the overall HWBI.

The relative importance of domains may vary for each person. Use the boxes to rank the importance of domains based on your preferences. A value of 5 is considered most important and a value of 1 has lowest importance.

County-Calculated Services
Modified Services and Weights

The services scores are used to calculate the eight domains of well-being, represented as eight sections on the pie chart. The relative importance values are represented by the width of pieces on the pie chart and used to calculate the overall HWBI score. The total Human Well-Being Index (HWBI) score is shown in the center of the pie chart.
